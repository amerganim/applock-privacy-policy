LockApp — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

LockApp ("the app") is an app locker that lets you protect selected apps on your device with a PIN, pattern, or biometric (fingerprint/face). Your privacy matters: LockApp is built to work entirely on your device.

Information we collect

None. LockApp does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There is no account, no sign-in, and no analytics or advertising SDK.

Data stored on your device

  • Your PIN / pattern and recovery answer are stored only as salted SHA-256 hashes inside Android's encrypted storage (EncryptedSharedPreferences). The actual secret is never stored and never leaves your device.
  • The list of apps you choose to lock and your settings (theme, auto-lock timing, etc.) are stored locally in private app storage.

All of this data is removed when you uninstall the app.

Permissions and why they are used

  • Usage access — to detect which app is currently in the foreground so LockApp can show the lock screen when a protected app opens. This information is used in memory only and is never recorded or transmitted.
  • Display over other apps — to show the lock screen on top of a protected app.
  • Query installed apps — to show you the list of apps you can choose to lock.
  • Notifications & foreground service — to keep the protection service running reliably and show its status.
  • Biometric — to offer fingerprint/face unlock. Biometric matching is handled entirely by the Android system; LockApp never receives your biometric data.
  • Camera (optional) — only if you enable "Intruder selfie," to take a front-camera photo after repeated wrong unlock attempts. These photos are saved only in LockApp's private storage on your device, are never uploaded, and can be deleted by you at any time.
  • Photos & videos / media access (optional) — only if you turn on "Remove original after import" in the vault, so LockApp can delete the original copies from your gallery (after you confirm in Android's own deletion dialog) once they have been moved into the encrypted vault. Media is never uploaded.
  • Device administrator (optional) — only if you enable "Tamper protection," to make the app harder to uninstall. You can disable it at any time.

Children's privacy

LockApp does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.
